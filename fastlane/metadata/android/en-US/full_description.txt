<p>Acastus-Photon translates name of street/city/point-of-interest
to geo-coordinate which can be</p>
<ul>
<li>opened in a map or navigation app such as OsmAnd, Maps.me, APhotoManager.</li>
<li>shared with other people via the send command.</li>
</ul>
<p>You can start Acastus by clicking on an adresss in the Android Contacts app (SimpleContatcs).</p>
<p>The Photon-Server-Software which Acastus-Photon calls over the internet
is a replacement for Google Maps and other geocoding services that you
can run on your own server.</p>
<p>Details on setting up your own Photon instance are here: https://github.com/komoot/photon
.</p>
